EXPANSE

Beautiful Realtime Volumetrics in Unreal Engine

Get Started

Create amazing volumetric environments in minutes.

Expanse is a real-time renderer for Unreal Engine 5 that revolutionizes the way creators work with volumetric content. Bring your digital experience to life with seamlessly integrated clouds, tornadoes, explosions, and any other animated volumetrics you can imagine.

Unparalleled Interaction

Create environments fearlessly with volumes that cast and receive shadows, composite against transparents, and integrate seamlessly with Lumen GI.

Native Open VDB Support

Build everything from moody fog-scapes to apocalyptic supercells out of overlapping VDB's with simple drag-and-drop workflows.

More Than Just Clouds

Dazzle your audience with true volumetric fog, fire, atmospheres, and explosions.
